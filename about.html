Our Story

From a single product listing to a marketplace people trust

We opened our first listing in 2016 selling refurbished headphones out of a spare bedroom. The photos were blurry, the descriptions were vague, and we promised ourselves we'd fix both before we listed anything else.

Nine years later, that promise is still the whole business model: fewer categories done properly beats a catalog nobody can trust. Every product on Stackly is checked against the same sourcing standard we used on day one.

What drives us

Mission, vision & values

Three ideas guide every decision, from what we sell to how fast we ship it.

Our Mission

Make genuine, well-tested electronics affordable and easy to find, without the guesswork that usually comes with buying tech online.

Our Vision

Become the marketplace people default to when a product actually needs to work on arrival, not just look good in a photo.

Our Values

Honest listings, fair pricing, and a support team that picks up the phone instead of hiding behind a ticket queue.
